Spend From Vault

This page spends from an existing 2/3-multisig smart contract using 2 Trezors.

You and your co-signer will need a total of 2 Trezor hardware wallets. Both Trezors do not need to be co-located. Your co-signer can use this application on their own computer and send you their signature via a (preferably secure) side-channel such as email.
